About Silva Iron Works

Silva Iron Works is a trusted iron and steel fabrication company serving the Bronx, Manhattan, and the greater New York area. We specialize in railings, gates, fences, fire escapes, structural steel, and custom ornamental metalwork for residential and commercial clients. Quality craftsmanship and customer satisfaction are at the core of everything we do.
