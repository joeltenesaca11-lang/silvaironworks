About Us

Silva Iron Works is a trusted iron and steel fabrication company serving the Bronx, Manhattan, and the greater New York area. We specialize in custom railings, gates, fences, fire escapes, structural steel, and ornamental metalwork for both residential and commercial clients.

With years of hands-on experience in welding and fabrication, our team delivers quality craftsmanship on every project — from a simple handrail repair to a full commercial steel installation. We are fully licensed and insured, and we stand behind every piece of work we produce.

Objective:

- Deliver superior iron and steel work that stands the test of time.
- Be known for craftsmanship, reliability, and fair pricing.

Mission:

- Combine traditional metalworking skills with modern techniques.
- Provide safe, code-compliant installations with outstanding customer service.

Our Services Include:

•  Iron Railings   •  Steel Gates   •  Iron Fences   •  Window Guards
•  Fire Escapes   •  Structural Steel   •  Welding & Repairs   •  Custom Fabrication
•  Ornamental Iron   •  Staircase Railings   •  Security Doors   •  Balconies
•  Cellar Doors   •  Metal Awnings   •  Steel Beams   •  Lally Columns
•  Rolling Gates   •  Iron Doors   •  Spiral Staircases   •  Metal Handrails
