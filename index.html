About Us

Silva Iron Works specializes in custom iron and steel fabrication for residential and commercial properties throughout the Bronx, Manhattan, and the greater New York area. With years of experience in ornamental and structural metalwork, our skilled welders and fabricators deliver quality craftsmanship on every project.

We take pride in our attention to detail and our commitment to building lasting relationships with our clients.

Objective:

- Deliver superior iron and steel work that stands the test of time.
- Be known for craftsmanship, reliability, and fair pricing.

Mission:

- Combine traditional metalworking skills with modern techniques.
- Provide safe, code-compliant installations with outstanding customer service.

Our Services Include:

•  Iron Railings   •  Steel Gates   •  Iron Fences   •  Window Guards
•  Fire Escapes   •  Structural Steel   •  Welding & Repairs   •  Custom Fabrication
